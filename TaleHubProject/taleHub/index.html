FAQ's

TaleHub is a platform where you can read, share, and discover stories from people around the world. Our mission is to create a space for storytelling that fosters connection, understanding, and inspiration.

To submit your story, go to the "Writer Page," write your story, and then click the "Upload" button to submit it. Follow the prompts to complete your submission. Once submitted, our team will review your story for approval.

TaleHub is free to use for both readers and writers. We believe in making storytelling accessible to everyone. However, we do offer a premium membership with additional features for those who want to support our platform.
